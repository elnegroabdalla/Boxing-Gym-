Boxing Impact on Health

  • Weekly Doses of Boxing can help lengthen life expectancy by 12.8 Percent
  • Boxing is a high-intensity aerobic workout that boosts heart health, lowers blood pressure, and improves circulation.
  • A typical boxing session can burn 500–1,000+ calories per hour, helping with weight loss and fat reduction.
  • Regular sparring and conditioning improve both muscular and cardiorespiratory endurance.
